Academic Excellence

Good education is possible! It empowers individuals for a productive life, beyond just schooling. It’s holistic, challenging, and built with grit.

Why Care About Academic Excellence?

  • Enjoy a good education
  • Appreciate academic excellence
  • Embrace lifelong learning

Learning Practices for Excellence:

  1. Chunking – Break hard things into small parts.
  2. Retrieval Practice – Teach/talk what you want to learn.
  3. Focus and Diffuse – Alternate focus with rest time.
  4. Scaffolding – Seek help to engage in higher tasks.

Excellence Through Feedback:

  • Build relationships with your community
  • Appreciate what others do right
  • Coach with specific advice
  • Evaluate by showing impact on the team
  • Affirm value to the team

Receiving Feedback Effectively:

  1. Build relationships
  2. Appreciate the feedback
  3. Re-voice improvement areas
  4. Evaluate the feedback
  5. Revisit and iterate

How Good Education Leads to Success:

  • Opens doors to scholarships/fellowships
  • Can lead to innovation funding
  • Builds work ethic and integration of skills
  • Helps you thrive globally
